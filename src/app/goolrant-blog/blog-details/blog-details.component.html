Mobile Commerce (M-Commerce)

May 15,2022

More often e-Commerce and M-Commerce are mistaken for each other. Fundamentally speaking e-Commerce is all about the sale or purchase of goods and services over the internet. In stark contrast to e-Commerce, M-Commerce or Mobile commerce is an extended arm of e-Commerce wherein the transaction occurs online over a mobile device.

With the growing popularity of mobile apps and increased financial transactions over mobile devices, the viral trend of shopping goods or services via user-friendly mobile applications will not stop anytime soon.

This growing trend of M-commerce has posed new demands from the consumers' end which need immediate attention from intuitive mobile app developers who can better the UI/UX of their developed apps thereby simplifying the entire application use cases.
